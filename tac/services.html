Revenue Management & Pricing Strategy

The Problem

Hotels losing 15–30% potential revenue through static pricing, poor channel management, and reactive rate decisions. No yield management discipline. OTA commissions eating into margins.

What TAC Does

Implement dynamic pricing frameworks, optimize OTA channel mix, set up rate parity monitoring, build demand forecasting models, and train your revenue team on yield management best practices.

Business Impact

25–35% RevPAR improvement within 90 days. Reduced OTA dependency. Increased direct bookings. Clear pricing strategy that adapts to market demand automatically.

GOP & Profitability Optimization

The Problem

Properties running at decent occupancy but still unprofitable. Cost leakages across departments. No structured P&L analysis. Thin margins despite respectable top-line revenue.

What TAC Does

Deep P&L audit, cost centre restructuring, procurement optimization, energy cost reduction, labour cost modelling, and department-wise margin analysis with actionable recommendations.

Business Impact

12–18% GOP improvement. Identified cost savings of ₹15–30 lakhs annually for mid-size properties. Sustainable profitability framework that compounds over time.

Operations Excellence & Workflow Design

The Problem

Chaotic daily operations, inconsistent service delivery, breakdown in department coordination, high staff turnover, and recurring guest complaints about basic operational failures.

What TAC Does

Full operational audit, workflow redesign, department coordination protocols, shift management optimization, quality control systems, and performance tracking dashboards.

Business Impact

40% reduction in operational complaints. Faster room turnaround. Improved department coordination. Staff clarity on roles and expectations. Smoother day-to-day operations.

Training & Team Development Programs

What TAC Does

Customized training programs from frontline to leadership. Real-scenario based workshops. On-floor coaching. Department-specific skill building. Service culture development.

Business Impact

4,500+ professionals trained. Measurable improvement in service scores. Reduced staff turnover. Self-sufficient teams that maintain standards after our engagement ends.

Guest Experience & Review Management

End-to-end guest journey mapping, touchpoint optimization, review response strategy, complaint resolution frameworks, surprise-and-delight programs, and feedback analysis systems.

SOP Development & Implementation

The Problem

No standardized procedures. Service quality varies by shift, by person, by mood. No documentation for training new hires. Inconsistency is the only consistent thing.

What TAC Does

Department-wise SOP creation built from real hotel operations (not copied templates). Training integration. Compliance tracking. Regular review and update cycles built in.

Business Impact

Consistent service delivery across all shifts. 60% faster new hire onboarding. Clear accountability. SOPs that teams actually follow because they were built with them, not for them.
